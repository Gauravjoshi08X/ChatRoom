**Chat** **Room**

A chatting app with no procedure, just type in the website and start
chatting in the room with people with similar interests.

**Features**

* A chatroom which an individual creates and flags it. Other just join and start chatting.

* No login required.

* Works under 5km radius.

* Targets mainly an organizations like company, colleges etc.

* Specific word based room selection

* Room maker selects a word and other can join with that word to join that very room out of many.

* Soft notification for notifying time left at room disposal.

**Regulations**

* Not violent room closing. Asks if they want to continue chatting and creates a new one.
    - Asks if they want to join.

* Kicking feature to avoid toxic room.

    * A voting system to kick them out and let them not join that particular room.

    * 60% voting against him

* Rate limiting for reducing spam room creation 1 device 1 room for 1/2 hour.
